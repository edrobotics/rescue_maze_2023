Design ideas:
	Have a rescue kit holder (like a well) with an extended "backwards" edge. A rescue kit is in the well, it moves, the rescue kit drops out as another one drops onto the "backwards" edge. When the mechanism retracts, the rescue kit on the "backwards" edge is pushed into the well by the back wall. This works both with linear and rotational motion.
